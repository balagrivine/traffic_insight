What is Traffic Insight?

Traffic Insight lets you check traffic conditions in your area using just a phone. No smartphone or internet needed! Just dial a USSD code, and you'll get an SMS with the latest traffic updates.
Whether you're on the road or planning a trip, our service keeps you informed, so you can avoid delays and get where you need to be on time.
Stay ahead of traffic with our easy-to-use service.
